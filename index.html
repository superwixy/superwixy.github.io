    CSGO ELO calculator
  • You input your current rank
  • you input the results of your last 10 games
  • we take your information and compile it into a nice easy to read format
  • next we allow you to input each additional game you play, with detailed stats
    • Kills
    • assists
    • deaths
    • MVP's
  • then we take your information and match it up against a large repository of existing match data
  • we use this to calculate how close you are to a rank up, and things you might do to improve your rank
